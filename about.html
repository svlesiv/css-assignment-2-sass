About me

Short bio

2008-2011

My name is Svitlana Lesiv. In 2008 I graduated from Lviv Ivan Franko National University (Ukraine) with Master Degree in Japanese language and Literature. After that I worked as a teacher and translator of Japanese Language for 3 years and studied in Computer Academy "Step" (Ukraine) at the same time.

In 2011 after receiving associate degree in Computer Graphic and Design I moved to USA and start working as a freelancer in a computer graphic field.

2011 - 2016

While working as freelancer and doing mostly 2d graphic projects (photo editing, logo design, website design, animated cards, design of game characters), I studied 3d character rigging and sculpting of 3d characters.

Also during those years I and my husband started our own business, we founded company "3dLions" in Lviv (Ukraine). "3dLions" was a 3d printing company, where people could order to print custom made or 'ready-to-go' 3d models.

2016-2017

When I was studying character rigging I became interested in coding, specifically in Python language, which helps to automate processes in Autodesk Maya while doing rigging. Last spring (2017) I took 2 courses in Harvard Extension School: "Fundamentals of Website Development" and "Introduction to Programming with Python". I fell in love with programming concepts and start digging deeper into Website development programming.
